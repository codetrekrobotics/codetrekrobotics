Coding & Robotics Adventures for Young Innovators

For Schools & Parents

Schools & Districts

Partner with Codetrek Robotics to bring turnkey coding and robotics programs to your students. Options include in‑school enrichment, after‑school clubs, and short‑term project residencies aligned with your STEM goals.

Families

Families can enroll children in after‑school clubs, camps, and weekend workshops. Programs are designed for beginners and experienced coders alike, with clear pathways from basic coding to advanced robotics.

About Codetrek Robotics

Codetrek Robotics was founded by a middle school STEM teacher with over 12 years of experience teaching PLTW Flight and Space, Green Architecture, Magic of Electrons, and technology courses in Massachusetts and Connecticut. Programs blend classroom‑tested pedagogy with fun, hands‑on robotics and coding challenges.
